About Me (This website is just for testings)

Bridging Data Science and Real-World Applications

I am currently a PhD Candidate at Tsinghua University, specializing in Data Science and Information Technology. My research interests include machine learning, robotics, and visual sensing, with a focus on developing intelligent systems for real-world applications.

My work explores the intersection of robotics and visual perception, particularly in the area of robust sensor fusion for autonomous systems. I am passionate about advancing human-robot interaction and enabling robots to better understand and interact with complex environments.

Research Interests

Exploring the Future of Intelligent Systems

Visual and Tactile Sensing

Exploring computer vision physical sensing techniques for real-time scene understanding and object detection in dynamic environments. I focus on multi-modal data integration and improving the reliability of visual systems in challenging conditions.

OpenCV PyTorch SLAM

Human-Robot Interaction

Investigating intuitive and safe interaction mechanisms between humans and robots. My contributions include designing user-centric interfaces and developing algorithms that enable robots to interpret and respond to human intentions effectively.

ROS Unity Arduino

Sensing System Algorithms

Developing and applying advanced algorithms tailored for sensing systems to enhance robotic perception, decision-making, and adaptability. My work includes robust sensor fusion, deep learning for visual recognition, and reinforcement learning for autonomous navigation.
